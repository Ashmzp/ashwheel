## üéØ How to Use in Coolify

### Step 1: Go to Service Settings
1. **Coolify Dashboard** ‚Üí **Your Supabase Project**
2. **GoTrue Service** ‚Üí **Environment Variables**

### Step 2: Add Variables
1. Click **Add Variable**
2. **Name:** `GOTRUE_MAILER_TEMPLATES_RECOVERY`
3. **Value:** Copy entire minified HTML from above
4. Click **Save**

### Step 3: Repeat for Other Templates
- `GOTRUE_MAILER_TEMPLATES_CONFIRMATION`
- `GOTRUE_MAILER_SUBJECTS_RECOVERY`
- `GOTRUE_MAILER_SUBJECTS_CONFIRMATION`

### Step 4: Redeploy
1. **Save** all variables
2. **Redeploy** service
3. Check logs
